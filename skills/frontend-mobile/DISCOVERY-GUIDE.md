# Frontend Mobile — Discovery Guide

Execute every step. Do not skip. Do not assume.

---

## Step 1: Surface Assumptions

List at least 5 assumptions about the project. Present for confirmation **in detected language**:

1. **Platform**: iOS, Android, or both?
2. **Target OS versions**: Minimum iOS version, minimum Android SDK?
3. **Navigation pattern**: Tabs, stack, drawer, or custom?
4. **Online/offline**: Always online, offline-first, or hybrid?
5. **Distribution**: App Store, Play Store, enterprise, or internal?

## Step 2: Extended Discovery (Mandatory)

Ask at least 5 of these. Do NOT skip if the answer seems obvious:

1. **Devices**: Phone only, or tablet + foldable?
2. **Auth**: Login system, social auth, biometric, or none?
3. **Push notifications**: Needed? Which provider (FCM, APNs)?
4. **Native features**: Camera, GPS, contacts, file system, health data?
5. **Payments**: In-app purchases, subscriptions, or none?
6. **Offline strategy**: Cache, local DB, conflict resolution?
7. **App Store requirements**: Screenshots, privacy labels, age rating?
8. **Design language**: iOS HIG, Material Design 3, or custom?
9. **Brand**: Existing colors, fonts, or start from scratch?
10. **Accessibility**: VoiceOver (iOS), TalkBack (Android), dynamic type?

## Step 3: Confirm

Summarize findings. Ask: "Is this correct? Shall we proceed?" Only after explicit yes.
